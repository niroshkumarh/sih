RiG - Smart India Hackathon Funding Portal!

Are you ready to transform your innovative ideas into reality? Do you have a passion for solving real-world problems using technology? Join us in this exciting opportunity to receive funding, mentorship, and ideation support for your project in the Smart India Hackathon!

Why Apply?

  • Funding Support: Get the financial backing you need to turn your project into a success. We believe in your potential and are here to invest in your innovative ideas.
  • Expert Mentorship: Learn from industry leaders and experts who will guide you through the technical and strategic aspects of your project. This mentorship is invaluable for honing your skills and expanding your knowledge.
  • Ideation & Team Formation: Whether you have a fully-fledged idea with a team or just a concept, we’re here to support you. If you're struggling to find a team, we’ll connect you with like-minded peers who share your vision.
  • Networking Opportunities: Be a part of a vibrant community of innovators and tech enthusiasts. This is your chance to network, collaborate, and learn from the best in the field.
  • Real-World Impact: Your project could be the next big solution that addresses some of the most pressing challenges in our society. This is your chance to make a tangible difference!

Application Categories

  • Complete Team with Idea: Have a problem statement and a solution ready? Submit your proposal along with the details of your team members. Each team can have up to 5 members.
  • Idea Without a Team: Got an idea but no team? No worries! Submit your idea, and if it’s promising, we’ll help you form a team with the right skills to bring your concept to life.
  • Team Without an Idea: Have a team but no specific idea yet? Submit your team details and skillsets, and we’ll match you with an exciting project idea that needs your expertise.

Steps to Selection

Step 1: Application Submission (Deadline: September 5)

Submit your application in the relevant category through the online portal. Ensure all required fields are filled out, including your problem statement, solution pitch, and team details.

Step 2: Initial Screening

Our panel of experts will review all applications to ensure they meet the eligibility criteria. Applications will be evaluated based on innovation, feasibility, and potential impact.

Step 3: Shortlisting

Shortlisted teams or individuals will be invited to present their ideas in more detail. If you're applying without a team, this is where we help you form one.

Step 4: Mentorship and Ideation Phase

Selected teams will be paired with mentors who are experts in the field. This phase will include workshops, brainstorming sessions, and guidance to refine your idea.

Step 5: Final Selection

After the mentorship phase, teams will present their refined proposals. The final selection will be based on the progress made, the viability of the project, and its potential impact.

Step 6: Funding and Development

The best projects will receive funding and continued support to develop and implement their solutions.

Important Dates

  • Application Deadline: September 5
  • Shortlisting Announcement: September 15
  • Mentorship Phase: September 20 - October 10
  • Final Selection: October 20
